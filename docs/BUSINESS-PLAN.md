# 💼 Business Plan — AprilDawn

> A condensed operating plan for the everything store for memories. Numbers are
> illustrative launch assumptions, not guarantees — validate before relying on them.

## 1. Market

- **Photo digitization & restoration** is a large, fragmented market driven by an aging population racing to preserve fading media (Legacybox, ScanCafe, local shops).
- **Personalized print-on-demand gifts** is a multi-billion-dollar category (Shutterfly, Printful-powered stores, Etsy makers).
- **Greeting cards & photobooks** remain durable; "smart" cards with embedded media are a fresh niche.
- **AprilDawn's wedge:** nobody owns the _full_ emotional journey — rescue → reimagine → remember — under one trusted, delightful brand with a lifetime vault and auto-gifting flywheel.

### Target customers
1. 👩‍👧 **The Family Archivist** (35–60) — inherits boxes, wants them safe and shareable.
2. 🎁 **The Thoughtful Gifter** — never wants to miss a birthday; loves personal gifts.
3. 🕊️ **The Memorial / Legacy** segment — funerals, estates, milestone celebrations.
4. 🏢 **B2B** — funeral homes, genealogists, senior communities, museums.

## 2. Offering & revenue model

| Stream | Examples | Margin profile |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| 🧾 **Per-project services** | Digitizing, restoration, prints, masterpieces | High on digital, medium on physical |
| 🔁 **Subscriptions** | Family Vault ($9/mo), card plans, Legacy | High, recurring, sticky |
| 🧱 **High-ticket installs** | Living Wall ($1.5k–$5k+) | Medium, premium AOV |
| 🛒 **Affiliate & partner** | Amazon Associates, maker markup | Pure-margin add-on |

### Illustrative pricing
- Digitizing: from **$0.39/scan** (volume-tiered).
- Restoration: from **$19/photo**; remaster/up-res from **$29**.
- Prints: tees from **$24**, canvas from **$39**, mugs from **$16**, cake prints from **$14**.
- Living Wall: **$1,499 (Mantel)** / **$2,900 (Gallery)** / **Custom (Heirloom)**.
- Memory Mail: cards from **$7**, photobooks from **$39**, card plans from **$12/mo**.
- Family Vault: **$9/mo** (20% off everything + auto-gifting + priority).

## 3. Unit economics (illustrative)

- **Digitizing box** AOV ~$120, blended gross margin ~55% after labor + return shipping.
- **Restoration** AOV ~$45, gross margin ~70% (AI-assisted, artist-finished).
- **Print order** AOV ~$38, gross margin ~45% (POD partner COGS).
- **Living Wall** AOV ~$2,400, gross margin ~40% (materials + LED + install).
- **Subscription** LTV driver: vault lock-in + recurring gifting; target <$30 CAC via referral + social.

> The flywheel: a digitizing customer becomes a vault subscriber, who becomes a
> repeat gifter, who refers family, who each bring _their_ shoebox.

## 4. Go-to-market

1. **Emotional social content** (before/after restorations, gag-gift reveals) on TikTok/Instagram/Pinterest — inherently shareable.
2. **SEO** on high-intent terms ("VHS to digital", "restore old photo", "photo on cake").
3. **Occasions engine as growth loop** — reminders bring users back; gifts get seen by recipients who become customers.
4. **Referral program** — give a free scan pack, get one.
5. **B2B partnerships** — funeral homes, senior living, genealogy societies.
6. **Affiliate/influencer** — makers and family creators.

## 5. Operations
- **Lab**: intake → inventory (photographed) → digitize → restore → QA proof → fulfill → return originals insured.
- **Fulfillment**: in-house for core prints; POD partners (Printful/Printify/Gooten) for the long tail; Amazon Associates for accessories.
- **Quality bar**: free proofs, 100% happiness guarantee, item-level tracking.

## 6. Key risks & mitigations
| Risk | Mitigation |
| :-- | :-- |
| Losing/damaging irreplaceable originals | Item-level tracking, insurance, careful handling SLAs |
| Copyright/likeness on prints | Rights attestation, moderation, consent gating (see LEGAL-COMPLIANCE) |
| Thin POD margins | Subscriptions + high-ticket installs + affiliate to balance |
| AI restoration over-promising | Always proof-gated; never auto-ship AI output |
| Seasonality (holidays) | Subscriptions + year-round occasions smooth revenue |

## 7. Milestones
- **M0:** Marketing MVP live (this repo) → collect leads & demand signal.
- **M1:** Checkout + vault + one fulfillment partner → first real revenue.
- **M2:** Restoration pipeline + occasions engine → retention & flywheel.
- **M3:** Living Wall + Memory Mail studios → premium AOV & differentiation.
