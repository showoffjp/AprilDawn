# 📜 Vision — AprilDawn

> _The everything store for memories._

## The problem

Our most precious moments are dying quietly. Photos fade and tear in drawers. VHS and camcorder tapes degrade a little more every year. Slides yellow. Voicemails get deleted by storage limits. And even our digital photos — 10,000 of them on a cracked phone — are one drop away from gone.

Meanwhile, the things people _want_ to do with those memories are scattered across a dozen mediocre services: one site scans, another restores, a third prints mugs, a fourth mails cards. Nobody owns the whole emotional journey from **shoebox → keepsake → forever**.

## The promise

AprilDawn is one warm, delightful place where any memory, in any form, can be:

1. 🛟 **Rescued** — digitized and transcribed from any format ever made, and restored from any damage time has done.
2. 🎁 **Reimagined** — printed on literally anything, painted into a masterpiece, or built into a glowing wall.
3. ♾️ **Remembered** — kept in a lifetime vault, mailed as talking cards, and auto-gifted so no occasion is ever missed.

## Why "AprilDawn"?

April is renewal; dawn is the light returning. The brand is about **bringing memories back into the light** — and the warm, hopeful feeling of seeing a lost moment restored. The mark is a rising sun over a horizon.

## North star metric

**Memories made permanent** — the count of irreplaceable items digitized, restored, and safely vaulted. Every other metric (revenue, retention, referrals) follows from doing this beautifully.

## Principles

- 🤲 **Memories are sacred.** Treat every item like the only copy — because it often is.
- 🪞 **Honest by default.** Free proofs, clear pricing, real disclosures.
- 🌅 **Joy, not just utility.** Happy tears _and_ gag gifts. Preserving the past should feel wonderful.
- 🔐 **Your data is yours.** Own your files, control integrations, export or delete anytime.

## What success looks like

A family mails in nine boxes from the attic, gets back a searchable, restored album; hangs a Living Wall the whole family adds to from their phones; and every birthday after that, AprilDawn quietly makes sure the perfect, personal gift shows up on time — for the rest of their lives.
